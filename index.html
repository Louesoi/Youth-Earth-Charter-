The Earth Charter of Youth Cards were born as a collective and symbolic process of re-creating the Earth Charter, 25 years after its original version. Young people from different regions of Brazil and around the world came together in 2025 to reflect on its principles in the face of the climate crisis, inequalities, and current ways of life.

The result is this youth deck of the Earth Charter, which translates the 16 principles into four themes:

Affection and community
Territory
Resistance and justice
Future and imagination

An educational and artistic tool, it expresses the power of youth in the search for more dignified, supportive, and sustainable ways of life. Join us in the commitment to defend life in all its forms!
